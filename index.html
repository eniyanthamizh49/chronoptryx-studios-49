About Chronoptryx Studios

Our Story

Founded with a passion for creating immersive gaming experiences, Chronoptryx Studios has been developing innovative 2D and 3D games for over 2 years. Our small but dedicated team of 2 professionals brings together expertise in game design, programming, and digital art.

We specialize in using industry-standard tools like Unity Engine for game development and Blender for 3D modeling and animation, ensuring our games meet the highest quality standards.
